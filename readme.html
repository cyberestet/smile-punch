Smile punch 👊😀

A game that plays entirely in the browser address bar !

Play ===> https://cyberestet.github.io/smile-punch/

Controls 🗿

Movement/Direction

| A | or | ← | for going left

| D | or | → | for going right

Punches

| , | 🤛 left

| . | 🤜 right

Each consumes 💪10

Powers

Hold | K | and direction to use the 🔥 jutsu when the 🔥 is 100

Hold |↑ Shift | and direction to jump over 1 enemy(ground). Each consumes 💪25
